2
How Your Data Flows

When you tap Send on the letter-compose screen, here is the exact sequence:

  1. Your reflection text, your first name, your selected emotion, and the Bible verse text are sent over HTTPS to our Firebase Cloud Function (hosted by Google in the United States).
  2. Our server performs a crisis keyword check on your reflection text. If crisis language is detected, the request stops here and no letter is generated. Your text is not forwarded to Anthropic.
  3. If no crisis content is detected, the data is forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your letter. The exact fields sent to Anthropic are: your first name, your emotion label, the Bible verse, and your reflection text (if provided).
  4. Anthropic returns the generated letter to our Firebase server, which immediately returns it to your device. We do not store your reflection text or the generated letter on our servers.
  5. The letter is displayed on your device. If you tap Save letter, the letter and your reflection text are saved in encrypted local storage on your device only.
Our Firebase server briefly processes your reflection text solely for the purpose of crisis detection and forwarding to Anthropic. This text is not written to any database or log by us. Firebase may capture metadata (function execution timestamps, error codes) but not the content of your submission.

4
Local Storage — What Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage (standard encrypted mobile storage). We cannot access, read, or retrieve any of this data:

  • Your emotion check-in history (emotions selected, timestamps)
  • All saved Bible verses
  • AI-generated letters (if you choose to save them)
  • Your reflection text ("What's on your heart") — saved locally alongside the letter if you tap Save
  • App preferences (theme, notification settings, haptic settings)
  • Onboarding and disclaimer completion status
  • Your subscription tier (mirrored locally from RevenueCat for fast UI rendering)

This data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app or use Start over in Settings.

6
Guest Mode

You may use the app as a guest without creating an account. In guest mode, no account credentials are collected or sent to Firebase. All app data (emotions, check-in history, preferences) is stored locally on your device only. Guest mode does not prevent the crisis keyword check if you use the letter feature — that check occurs on our server regardless of account status.

If you later create an account, your local data remains on your device and is not migrated to or linked with your account.

7
Crisis Keyword Detection
This is not a crisis monitoring service. The App does not monitor your usage in real time or employ human reviewers.

When you submit reflection text to generate a letter, our server automatically checks whether the text contains certain crisis-related phrases. This is a one-time automated check performed at the moment of submission. If crisis content is detected, the server returns crisis resource information (such as 988 and the Crisis Text Line) instead of generating a letter. Your text is not forwarded to Anthropic in this case.

This feature is provided solely as a safety measure. It does not constitute crisis counseling, mental health monitoring, or real-time intervention. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a qualified professional immediately.

Letters are generated by Anthropic's Claude AI model. Anthropic processes the data we send — your first name, emotion, verse, and reflection text — in accordance with their Privacy Policy. Anthropic may retain submitted content and use it for model improvement unless you exercise opt-out rights directly with Anthropic.

We do not store generated letters on our servers. Letters exist only on your device if you choose to save them.
